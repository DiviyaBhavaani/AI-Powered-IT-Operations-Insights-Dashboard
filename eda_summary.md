# Exploratory Data Analysis

## Dataset at a glance
- Total incidents: **3,500**
- Closed incidents: **2,968**
- Open incidents: **532**
- Overall SLA breach rate: **43.4%**
- Closed-incident SLA breach rate: **33.4%**
- Average closed-incident resolution time: **30.66 hours**
- Median closed-incident resolution time: **22.97 hours**

## Key findings
1. **Application** has the highest closed-incident breach rate at **54.7%**.
2. **Network** has the lowest closed-incident breach rate at **16.9%**.
3. The team with the largest open backlog is **SRE** with **69** open incidents.
4. **North America** has the highest closed-incident breach rate among regions at **34.5%**.
